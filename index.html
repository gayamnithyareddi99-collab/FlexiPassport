PROBLEM → SOLUTION

From Compliance Chaos
to Trusted Digital Identity

The Problem

Today’s workforce identity and compliance systems are fragmented, manual, and error-prone. Workers are repeatedly vetted, agencies store outdated documents, and organisations carry enormous legal and financial risk due to missed expiries and compliance gaps.

Regulations like Right to Work and the AWR 12-week rule are enforced reactively often after damage is done exposing businesses to heavy penalties and operational delays.

→

The FlexiPassport Solution

FlexiPassport transforms identity into a living, portable, and continuously verified digital asset. Workers hold their own verified credentials, while organisations instantly validate compliance through secure, automated workflows.

By combining Self-Sovereign Identity, automated verification, and real-time compliance tracking, FlexiPassport replaces paperwork with trust and risk with confidence.
